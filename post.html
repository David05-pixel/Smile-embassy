How To Keep Your Teeth Healthy Every Day

By Smile Embassy • May 2026

Good oral hygiene is not complicated, but it must be consistent. Small daily habits can protect your teeth for life.

1. Brush Twice Daily

Use a soft toothbrush and fluoride toothpaste. Brush for at least 2 minutes every morning and night.

2. Floss Every Day

Flossing removes plaque and food particles between teeth that brushing cannot reach.

3. Visit Your Dentist Regularly

A dental check-up every 6 months helps prevent serious dental problems before they worsen.

4. Reduce Sugar Intake

Too much sugar increases the risk of cavities and gum disease.

5. Drink More Water

Water helps wash away bacteria and food particles while keeping your mouth hydrated.
