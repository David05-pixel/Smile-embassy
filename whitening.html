Whitening, done the right way.

Years of coffee, tea, soft drinks, and red wine leave their mark on your teeth.

Over-the-counter whitening kits are slow, often painful, and rarely give the result you want.

Our in-clinic whitening treatment uses dental-grade whitening agents applied by a trained dentist — lifting stains safely, evenly, and dramatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it hurt?

Most patients feel nothing. Some experience mild sensitivity for a day.

How long does it last?

Results typically last 1–3 years with proper care.

Can I whiten if I have crowns or veneers?

Yes, but whitening only affects natural teeth.
